About Me

I am Nick Nguyen and my focus is on the user experience. I disliked how video game bot scripts looked, and so for a period of time I built them myself to compensate. Using my developed skills in the front end I am able to create an enjoyable experience for the user.

Much has been said about creating a desireable user experience, but what makes me so confident I am able to create one? Well, for starters, I have many years working in customer service. Starting at 15 I had run an online market, getting user data and adjusting based on customer demands. I've worked in person with people of varying disabilities as well, which has developed my listening skills, allowing me to take feedback and adapt accordingly.

I might not be technically inclined as other people, but I know what I can do is work hard and work effectively, and that is all I can control. As they say, jealousy is the theif of joy.
